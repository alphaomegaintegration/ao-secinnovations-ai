Section 5 · Securing the Stack

Protected by Zscaler + red-teamed with SPLX.ai

Zscaler enforces zero-trust access to the network and apps; SPLX.ai proves the AI itself holds up under attack. Defense in depth — from the connection to the model.

Zscaler · Zero-Trust Access

🌐 Secure the connection

  • Zero-trust network access — users reach apps, never the network
  • Full SSL inspection & cloud firewall on all traffic
  • Inline DLP to stop sensitive filing & exam data exfiltration
  • Identity-driven policy with continuous posture checks
SPLX.ai · AI Red-Teaming

🛡️ Secure the model

  • Automated prompt-injection & jailbreak attacks
  • Data-exfiltration & PII/PHI leakage probes
  • Re-tests on every model or prompt change
  • Scored, audit-ready findings mapped to AI risk frameworks

Layered defense: Zscaler zero-trust → Cognito auth → Bedrock Guardrails → SPLX.ai red-team → audit archive. Not a single checkpoint.
